What I Do

Anthony Omojo Cletus is a Nigerian engineer, educator, and AI researcher with nearly a decade building and teaching software that empowers people. My work focuses on privacy, accessibility, and inclusive infrastructure, especially for communities with limited internet and digital protection.

I am the Cofounder of Programmify, the co-creator of Tendly, and the lead architect of AANI, an offline-first AI assistant designed for the Next Billion. I combine technical depth with strong product strategy, growth experience, and a commitment to ethical AI. I have taught at universities, led innovation teams, and received the United Nations Eminent Award for Peace and Reconciliation.
