About West Sunset NFT

This collection is a reflection of big dreams.
Dreams that have the tendency to impact positive change on the world.

It is believe that each time the sun sets in the west,
dreams are elevated and people are strengthened to make plans and work towards achieving their dreams.
But what is more important is that these dreams are actually realized.
The West Sunset is a sign of goodluck, progress
Do you have a big dream? Do you want to dream?

The West Sunset NFT is a unique digital photograph collection of the sun as it rest upon earth
and mankind living on the Ethereum blockchain.
A holder of the West Sunset has a strong will to bring their colourful dreams to reality.

The Specs

Each West Sunset is unique and has the sun setting upon the city with a male or female human posing in different styles.
West Sunset NFTs are stored as ERC-1155 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain. Purchasing are only done via OpenSea.
You can buy a West Sunset with any Ethereum supported crypto wallets.
